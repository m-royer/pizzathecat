A DAY IN MY LIFE

Morning:

I hate mornings! They make me angry! FEED ME!!!

My day starts at 7am when my human comes to my room and gives me a scoop of food! Sometimes they say hello, but they usually have other things to do I guess! I used to stress out about that, but I am an older, wiser cat now.

Daytime:

This is my favorite time of day. The human that usually pets me is around, and I looooove pets. They make me happy!

Sometimes I get a little bit cranky when I'm not getting attention (which must be at all times) and I need the comfort of my safety vest. It keeps me calm and visible!

Evening:

Evening time is when my regular humans are back from work. My favorite thing during this time is to sing the song of my people and sitting on someone's lap in front of the fire and clawing the shit out of them (it brings me peace).

Nighttime:

During the night, my regular humans watch loud movies, which hurts my ears. That also usually means they're not paying attention to me, which is terrible! Instead I like to go hang out in my room, which is quiet, has my litterbox, and sometimes there will still be food from earlier. Yippie!!
